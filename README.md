Welcome to **e-plantShopping**, a comprehensive e-commerce platform dedicated to plant enthusiasts. 

## Project Overview
This application is designed to provide a seamless shopping experience for users looking to browse, select, and purchase a wide variety of plants. From indoor succulents to outdoor perennials, **e-plantShopping** aims to bring nature closer to your home through a modern web interface.

## Key Features
* **Product Catalog:** A diverse range of plants categorized for easy navigation.
* **Shopping Cart:** Efficient management of selected items before checkout.
* **Responsive Design:** Optimized for both desktop and mobile viewing.
